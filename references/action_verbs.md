# Resume action verbs

Lead every bullet with a strong, specific verb. Grouped by what they signal. Source: the
"power words" categorization popularized by novoresume's resume action-words guide
(novoresume.com/career-blog/top-10-most-powerful-action-words-for-resume).

Rule of thumb: one strong verb per bullet (two at most). Replace weak openers like
"Responsible for", "Worked on", "Helped with", "Tasked with" -- they describe duties, not impact.

## Leadership & management
Achieved, Attained, Strengthened, Initiated, Coordinated, Developed, Managed, Orchestrated,
Implemented, Recommended, Overhauled, Improved, Streamlined, Prioritized, Supervised, Delegated, Chaired

## Teamwork & communication
Advised, Advocated, Clarified, Corresponded, Defined, Gathered, Informed, Interpreted, Persuaded,
Aided, Enabled, Encouraged, Facilitated, Fostered, Supported, Collaborated, Guided, Instructed, Mentored

## Analysis & research
Analyzed, Assessed, Examined, Explored, Evaluated, Investigated, Reviewed, Tested, Tracked, Audited,
Budgeted, Calculated, Documented, Estimated, Verified, Detected, Discovered, Measured, Mapped, Surveyed

## Creativity & building
Conceptualized, Designed, Devised, Drafted, Formulated, Introduced, Launched, Originated, Adapted,
Applied, Built, Customized, Integrated, Modified, Piloted, Shaped, Shipped, Prototyped, Pioneered

## Problem-solving & improvement
Debugged, Diagnosed, Fixed, Rectified, Reconciled, Rebuilt, Upgraded, Corrected, Maintained,
Operated, Refined, Simplified, Standardized

## Results & efficiency (great for money/time framing)
Amplified, Boosted, Converted, Cut, Elevated, Grew, Lifted, Raised, Reduced, Redesigned,
Reorganized, Restructured, Saved, Scaled, Slashed, Accelerated, Automated, Delivered

## Banned even though some lists include them
Avoid cliches that read as filler or over-claiming regardless of the list above:
**spearheaded, leveraged, passionate, results-oriented, proven track record, synergies, go-getter,
think outside the box, detail-oriented** (show it instead of claiming it).
